What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work or improve their efficiency, as well as to provide information to website owners.

Analytics Cookies: We use analytics cookies to analyze how visitors use our website, track user interactions, and gather information about the performance of the website. This helps us improve our website and provide a better user experience.
Preference Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and settings, such as language preferences, font size, and other customizable elements of the website.
